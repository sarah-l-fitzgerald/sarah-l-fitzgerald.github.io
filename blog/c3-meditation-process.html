Meditation Process

How did the process over product concept affect the way you tackled the site design and rebuild?

The process over product concept really helped me focus on achieving this project. By focussing on the steps of the project - I was able to complete it even when I knew from a visual perspective the site did not look how I had wanted. If I hadn't focused on the process then I may have become so mired down I would not have been able to produce anything functional.

What did you think about meditation before reading chapter 2?

Meditation has been part of my life in different ways for the last five years. I have a friend who has been practising for over thirty years and we have had many discussions around what it is and what it can do for wellness. Around three years ago I decided to undertake a transendental meditation course to alleviate severe anxiety. Chapter 2 outlines meditation in an almost identiical way to transendental meditation - the difference beign in the length of time and the process of coming out of the meditation.

What new things have you learnt about meditation?

My knowledge of meditation has not changed by reading the book, however I was interested in the link to happiness. Initially after reading the declaration that meditation can deliver you happiness I was sceptical but I realised that the happiness that Cheng is speaking of is not the &ldquo popular &rdquo idea of happiness pertpetuated in the media but a deep knowledge of yourself.

Did any of the meditation techniques stand out to you?

The mindfulness meditation closely mirrors the process of transendental meditation with the difference being that a mantra is used in transendental meditation that is particular to each individual. The mantra helps to us to just be in the moment.
